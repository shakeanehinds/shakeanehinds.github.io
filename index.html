My Passion

The advent of technology has definitely had a positive impact on our world, from making education more available through the use of online schools to leveraging tech for solving world crises and health problems. I enjoy being apart of this positive movement, my dream is to improve the lives of our people through the use of technology, making tech more accessible to those with disabilities and improving the

experience people have when they interface with software. By doing my part as a software developer I want to breathe life into ideas, build amazing user experiences and develop software solutions that will literally shape the future. Striking the balance between visually captivating and near-perfect execution is my end goal of becoming an amazing designer/developer.
