Here's a story

I grew up in the countryside of Jamaica, however I was schooled in the city and exposed to computers at an early age. I have always been fascinated about the science behind how rockets made it to space and the laws behind how our world operated. My introduction to computer science made me more intrigued by how computers operated and how they knew what to do. Overtime my fascination turned into a passion and that's how I got here. Solving problems and orchestrating great experiences with each line of code and brush stroke.

My Passion

The advent of technology has definitely had a positive impact on our world, from making education more available through the use of online schools to space exploration and leveraging tech for solving world crises and health problems. I enjoy being apart of this positive movement, my dream is to improve the lives of our people through the use of technology.

Making tech more accessible to those with disabilities and improving the experience people have interfacing with software. As a software developer I want to breathe life into ideas, build amazing user experiences and develop software solutions that will literally shape the future. Striking the balance between visually captivating and near-perfect execution is my end goal of becoming an amazing designer/developer.

Focus Areas

UX Design

My main focus is User Experience design, I have been honing my skills by improving existing systems in my local space, through employing proven UX design principles. With every decision made or route taken when creating a product, the end user should be in mind. This is how we ensure our products are really solving the problem, or fulfilling a need. By delivering an exceptional experience in the end, we differentiate okay products from remarkable ones.

Development

I develop mobile and web applications as a UI developer, mainly using Javascript frameworks such as Vue.js and React Native. However, I use other technologies as required by projects. I'm constantly improving on my skills as a developer as I appreciate being able to build the designs I produce or any ideas that I may have to solve a problem through the use of code. Who says you can't be the farmer and the chef ?
